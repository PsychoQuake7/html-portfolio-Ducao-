About Me

In addition to web development, I have a growing interest in Artificial Intelligence and hope to pursue deeper studies in AI in the future. I am fascinated by how intelligent systems can be used to improve accessibility, automate processes, and create innovative solutions for society.

Working on this project has strengthened my appreciation for technology's potential to make a positive impact on people's lives while allowing me to apply both my technical and research skills.

Experiencing different environments, cultures, and perspectives inspires me and helps broaden my outlook on life. When it comes to food, one of my favorites is the well-loved Filipino noodle dish, Pancit Canton. Overall, I am a curious learner, passionate developer, and technology enthusiast who is always eager to grow, explore new opportunities, and contribute meaningful solutions through computing and innovation.
